Services And Amenities

At Victoria Nile Suites, we offer a number of special amenities and a diversity of services , these include:

  1. Rooms and Suites
    All the rooms are well organised, furnished and clean for a fun and pleasant experience, from the ordinary to the presidential suites.
  2. Continental dishes and Buffets
    We prepare local and continental dishes, buffets with all day round meals and snacks available.
  3. Fitness centre and Sauna
    At Victoria Nile Suites, we do also prioritize fitness and healthy living. With our well-equipped, clean and spacious gyms, with instructors in place for assistance and all gym accessories, we’ve got you covered.
  4. Conference halls and rendezvous
    Whether it’s a family, wedding, business or any other very important meeting, Victoria Nile Suites has got it all catered for. With our well furnished and extremely organized halls, the quiet and peaceful space, the lightning-fast free Wi-Fi and the delicious meals, your meeting is guaranteed to yield the best of your expectations.
  5. The diverse accessibility features for people with disabilities.
    We are committed to providing the best experiences to people of all nations, origins and walks of life. This is why we have well designed accessibility features dedicated to people with disabilities, so that they are assisted.
  6. Our pet policy
    Pets are not allowed at the hotel regardless of whether they are healthy, well immunised or on a leash or pet lead.
  7. Health policy
    Our health policy ensures that all of our clients are well taken care of and are bound to receive the best of care from the management and staff.
