FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) And Reviews

What is so special about this Hotel?

Victoria Nile Suites is located along the banks of the Nile river, enabling all visitors to see and experience the beauty of the very astonishing and intriguing nature of the Nile, the tranquil sound of the water flowing, the cool and fresh breeze, the clean and fresh air sieved of all pollution by the thick grasses and canopies on the banks of the Nile, the breath taking and beautiful life force of the Nile, from the water inhabitants to the land dwelling and flying creatures, all on the Nile.

Is the Nile River safe for children and visitors?

At Victoria Nile Suites, safety is one of the utmost issues put in consideration at the premises. The premises are well guarded and monitored day and night, with guides in place to direct, attend to and assist all visitors in any way possible. There are safety measures in place to ensure safety of children of all ages and adults too, from guide posts, warning sign posts, language interpreters to an onsite medical centre with well trained doctors to attend to all emergencies and an ambulance to rush any victims or patients in very serious situations.

Is one allowed to come with there own food to the hotel?

As a precaution, no visitor or employee is allowed to enter with or carry any form of eatable whether a snack or cooked food. The hotel is well equiped with all kinds of foods and drinks, therefore every visitor is guranteed that they will find many of the different kinds of foods that they may need.

In case I cancel my booking, can my money be refunded?

At Victoria Nile Suites, to ensure customer satisfaction, we do have a policy that can enable you get a portion or all of your money refunded. This depends mainly on the time period when one has informed us of there termination of the booking.
