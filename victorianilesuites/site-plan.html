Overview

Purpose

Welcome to the source of the Nile, the grand jewel of the Pearl of Africa. A phenomena so intriguing, full of marvellous wonders, with a rich history and culture, a blessing to the nations of Africa and the rest of the world. We at Victoria Nile Tours are committed to providing utmost and fully satisfying care and comfort to the guests of the Nile. With this platform in place, it will be made easier for any of all our visitors or customers to fully view and understand the services we offer, have a glimpse and a brief feel and realization of how fun and adventurous it is to visit the source of the Nile. It is to provide a platform to allow and enable users make bookings in advance so that we, at the agency can get ready to receive, care for and accommodate our guests. It will also enable our online and previous visitors express their opinions regarding there experience at the Nile in Uganda, and suggest any changes we can make and how we can do it better.

Audience

At Victoria Nile Suites, is a diverse mix of cultures from nations all over the world. From people and families on vacation, business men and women to groups of tourists from the local areas and abroad, exquisite and satisfying services are in place that suite people of all classes and status and at the most affordable and friendly charges. There are also draws to offer discounts to the lucky ones that make bookings to have a feel of the stunning and intriguing sites and services we have to offer.

FAQ's and Reviews

 What is so special about this Hotel? Victoria Nile Suites is located along the banks of the Nile river, enabling all visitors to see and experience the beauty of the very astonishing and intriguing nature of the Nile, the tranquil sound of the water flowing, the cool and fresh breeze, the clean and fresh air sieved of all pollution by the thick grasses and canopies on the banks of the Nile, the breath taking and beautiful life force of the Nile, from the water inhabitants to the land dwelling and flying creatures, all on the Nile.  Is the Nile River safe for children and visitors? At Victoria Nile Suites, safety is one of the utmost issues put in consideration at the premises. The premises are well guarded and monitored day and night, with guides in place to direct, attend to and assist all visitors in any way possible. There are safety measures in place to ensure safety of children of all ages and adults too, from guide posts, warning sign posts, language interpreters to an onsite medical centre with well trained doctors to attend to all emergencies and an ambulance to rush any victims or patients in very serious situations.
